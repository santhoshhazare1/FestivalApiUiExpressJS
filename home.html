Festivals of India

Diwali

Diwali is a festival of lights. Diwali symbolises the spiritual "victory of light over darkness, good over evil and knowledge over ignorance."

Raksha Bandhan Or Rakhi

Raksha bandhan is a festival celebrating the eternal bond of brothers and sisters. In this festival, sister tie an auspicious thread to the brother's wrist, as a symbol of protection, that the brother bestows on her.
